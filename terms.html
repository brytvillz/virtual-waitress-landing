1. What Virtual Waitress Is

Virtual Waitress is a Software-as-a-Service (SaaS) platform that enables restaurants to create digital menus accessible to customers via QR codes. The platform includes:

  • A customer-facing digital menu (accessible by scanning a QR code — no account required)
  • A real-time ordering system that sends orders to restaurant staff
  • A waiter dashboard for managing and fulfilling incoming orders
  • A manager dashboard for restaurant administration and analytics
